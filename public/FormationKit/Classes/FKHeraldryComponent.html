FKHeraldryComponent

public class FKHeraldryComponent : GKComponent, FKButtonNodeProtocol

The icon that sits on top of the leader of a squad represents the HeraldryComponent. Primary features are:

  • Respond to touch and double touch
  • Use cropping to show health of squad around edges of icon
  • Keep itself positioned in the right place. Updates each frame.
  • If the unit is off of the screen, lock to the screen edges
  • Show status, selection and other effects.
