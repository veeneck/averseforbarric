FKModifierComponent

public class FKModifierComponent : GKComponent

Goal is to store all active modifiers. If a modifier is permanent, it will call AttributesComponenet to adjust the value. If temporary, it will just hold on to the modifier. Will take care of expiring Duration and DoT modifiers.
